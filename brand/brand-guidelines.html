01Brand Essence

Jeong AI builds AI systems, custom software, and automated workflows designed around how a specific business actually works — not generic SaaS sold to ten thousand companies at once.

Positioning statement

"Software is not one size fits all."

Brand personality

  • Professional — credible, precise, business-first.
  • Warm — human and approachable, never cold or corporate-jargony.
  • Reliable — an operational partner, not a subscription you forget about.

Internal north star: "Consistent presence. Genuine care. Quiet reliability."

02Logo System

The identity has two parts: the 정 brush mark — the Korean spelling of "Jeong," hand-rendered in calligraphy — and the Montserrat wordmark ("Jeong" bold 700 in Jeong Blue, "AI" medium 500 in muted gray). They combine into a small set of approved lockups, each with a defined role.

Rules

  • Mark color follows background: ink (or Jeong Blue) on light, white on dark. Never place the dark mark on a dark field.
  • Clear space: keep space around the lockup equal to the height of the 정 mark's lower loop.
  • Pairing: the mark sits to the left of (or centered above) the wordmark — at roughly 1.5× the wordmark's cap height. Don't shrink it below the cap height.
  • Don't: recolor "Jeong" to the accent, restyle the wordmark in another typeface, stretch or add effects to the brush mark, or rotate it.
  • The brush mark may be used alone as an icon (favicon, avatar, app tile) — preferably in the blue seal tile.

Usage ratio

Lead with blue and neutrals. Terracotta is an accent — reserve it for calls-to-action, accent rules, and section tags (roughly 10% of any layout). Purple is a secondary flavor for partnership/education contexts; don't mix it heavily with terracotta.

04Typography

Headings: Montserrat (400–700). Body: Source Sans 3 (300–600). Both from Google Fonts. Base body size 16–17px, line-height ~1.6.

05Buttons & Shape

Buttons use Montserrat 600, 2px borders, 6px radius. Primary buttons fill with Jeong Blue and shift to terracotta on hover.

Shape language

  • Corner radius: 6px default, 12px for cards/large surfaces.
  • Shadows are soft and low-opacity (never harsh) — sm / md / lg scale.
  • Accent rules: a short terracotta bar (~90px × 3px) under headers and section breaks.

06Voice & Tone

Direct, confident, and human. We speak to business owners, not engineers. Short sentences. Concrete benefits. No hype, no buzzword soup.

✓ Do

"We build tools that fit exactly how you work."

"Retain an operational partner, not a software subscription."

Lead with the customer's problem and outcome.

✕ Don't

"Leverage synergistic AI-driven paradigms."

Over-promise or use fear-based urgency.

Bury the point under technical jargon.
